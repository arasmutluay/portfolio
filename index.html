My Working Experience

I worked as an engineer at a crypto-mining farm specializing in GPU mining for 1.5 years. During my time at work, I took on the responsibility of maintaining and enhancing the operational efficiency of the graphics cards.

This was the "AMD floor" of the farm. It consisted of 24 rigs, each rig had 8 GPUs on it, making a total of 192 GPUs.

This was our second floor; it housed both NVIDIA cards, primarily RTX 3090 models, and AMD cards. This floor experienced the most issues because the NVIDIA cards were overheating due to inadequate stock fans and other variables. We had to replace fans, adjust overclocking settings, and select algorithms specific to the RTX 3090 cards. Eventually, we implemented a ventilation system for the farm since, at one point, temperatures were reaching 60°C.
